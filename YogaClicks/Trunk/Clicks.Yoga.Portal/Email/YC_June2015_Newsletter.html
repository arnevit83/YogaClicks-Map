Our YogaClicks family is expanding.
 
Meet our team:
L to R: Leah, Digital Marketing Manager - Duncan, Senior Developer - Lucy, Founder & Director - Ben, Developer
We're delighted that our team is growing, and so are our ideas. Our expansion will allow us to deliver super cool new features much, much faster, in order to make using YogaClicks unique, easy and rewarding. We couldn't be more excited.
 
Since YogaClicks has been my baby these past few years, I have had to adjust and grow with it. It felt foreign at first because I was so used to it being part of my own personal journey. By accepting new people and opening myself up to their ideas, this little baby is turning into a force to be reckoned with. I couldn't have got here on my own.
 
Yoga is a bit like that, isn't it? In class you flow together, you breathe together, and after everyone is warmed up, that moment happens. Everyone's in sync. The sound of the exhale is loud and determined. You cannot ignore it and you don't want to. Up Dog, Down Dog, Child's Pose. We're one #YogaHood under the groove.
 
That's what we hope YogaClicks is to you, a strong community of people working towards the same goal. We aim to connect you with like-minds, with people you can share ideas with, easily find in times of need, and help you realise that your journey means something to someone else, somewhere.
 
Warmest wishes and plentiful oms,
Lucy
